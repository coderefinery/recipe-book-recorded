# Mixed Nuts

## Ingredients

- 1 Red Pepper
- 1 Yellow Pepper
- 1 Green Pepper
- 3 tomatoes
- 1 Onion
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1 medium fish
- palm oil (azeite de dende)
- 2 limes

## Instructions

- marinate the fish for a day with the lime and green pepper
- cook it all together in a pan until fish is cooked and vegetables soft
